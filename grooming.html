How to Groom Your Pet at Home

Regular grooming is essential for your pet's health and happiness. A good brushing session not only removes dirt, dandruff, and dead hair, but also stimulates natural oils to distribute across the fur, giving it a healthy sheen.

  • Brushing: Use a slicker brush or bristle brush depending on the coat type. Brush gently in the direction of hair growth.
  • Bathing: Use pet-specific shampoo. Human shampoo can disrupt their skin's pH balance!
  • Nail Clipping: Clip nails regularly to prevent joint pain. Be careful not to cut the "quick" (the pink part containing blood vessels).
  • Ear Cleaning: Use cotton balls (never Q-tips) and an ear cleaner designed for pets.
